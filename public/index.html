Timestamp-Microservice
FreeCodeCamp API-Project By Nnenanya O.K

Root: https://timeserver-king.herokuapp.com/
GitHub: https://github.com/obinnaeye/timestamp

Supported Formats

Natural:

    .../2016-12-02
    .../02-12-2016
    .../December 2, 2016
    .../December 2 2016
    .../Dec 2, 2016
    .../Dec 2 2016
    .../2 December 2016
    .../2 December 16
    .../2 Dec 2016
    .../2 Dec 16

Unix:

    .../9876543210

Samples

Natural:

    https://timeserver-king.herokuapp.com/02-12-2016
Result: {Natural: Dec 2, 2016, Unix: 1480636800}

Unix:

    https://timeserver-king.herokuapp.com/1480636800
